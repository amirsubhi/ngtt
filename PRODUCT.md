# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Power users and serious media collectors who participate in private trackers. They access NGTT primarily on desktop, often in low-light conditions, and decide whether to sign in or request access within seconds based on perceived quality and scale.

## Product Purpose
NGTT is a private BitTorrent tracker community. It exists to provide a curated, high-availability content library with community-driven quality standards. Success means members trust it as their primary source and contribute actively.

## Brand Personality
Efficient, clean, fast. NGTT does not perform exclusivity — it demonstrates it through precision. Minimal ceremony, immediate value, zero noise.

## Anti-references
- Old-school tracker aesthetic: HTML tables, neon text, Pirate Bay-era design language
- Crypto/NFT darkness: aggressive neon-on-black, hype energy, laser-eye culture
- Generic SaaS landing: hero-metric templates, gradient CTA buttons, "10,000+ users" social proof blocks

## Design Principles
1. Prove quality, don't announce it — the interface is the argument
2. Immediate information, zero noise — every element earns its pixel
3. Dark and precise — the default experience is low-light, desktop, focused
4. Data as identity — stats and content are the brand, not marketing copy
5. One path, clearly lit — hierarchy guides action without shouting

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA minimum. Default dark theme; light themes (lumen, sand) available. RTL supported for Arabic locale.
